Welcome to Team Welly

Custom Wellness for Teams & Individuals

Team Welly is your all-in-one solution for modern wellness. Whether you're an HR leader supporting your team or an individual looking to improve performance, we've built the platform to help you move, recover, and thrive — every day.

Our Mission

We believe that when people feel better, they lead better — at home, at work, and everywhere in between.

Team Welly was created to make wellness actionable, measurable, and meaningful for both individuals and companies. Whether you're recovering from stress, aiming to move better, or leading a high-performing team, our platform helps you build sustainable routines that actually stick.

We're not just another wellness platform. We're a movement towards daily ownership of mind, body, and culture.

How It Works

Team Welly uses a proven 4-phase framework to guide every user:

  1. Onboarding – Set goals, take a baseline assessment, and sync your wearable devices
  2. Content Discovery – Explore stretch, breathwork, and movement routines based on your needs
  3. Daily Engagement – Track your wellness points, build consistency, and celebrate wins
  4. Social + Coaching – Join challenges, get 1-on-1 coaching, and build community
  5. Analytics + Growth – See your trends and progress over time

Our platform meets users where they are — and helps them go further.
